ABOUT

Bravo Dance Company

Salsa dancing in Vancouver has become more prominent in Vancouver’s entertainment and nightlife in response to the fast growing salsa community in the lower mainland. All year round you will now find a salsa night for every night of the week! Join our upcoming Summer salsa dancing courses and get on the dance floor! Bravo Dance Company is one of the hottest and most distinguished schools for salsa dancing in Vancouver, Canada and around the world! Our teaching technique represents the best of L.A. styles not to mention precise footwork and styling for both lead and follow.

instructor

Alfonso Caldera

Alfonso Caldera is a W.S.F. World Salsa Champion & International Mayan Salsa Champion dancer. He is the director and choreographer of Bravo Dance Company (Salsa dancing in Vancouver service) and founder of BravoDanceCompany.com.

Through the years, Alfonso competed in some of the top Salsa competitions where he consistently placed in the top three, including the Miami World Salsa Federation competition, winning the championship trophy.

Alfonso has developed a unique style of Salsa dancing, LA-style Salsa which is prevalent in his teaching technique both in his Salsa dancing classes in Vancouver, along with his instructional videos.

This coming Summer season, add some sizzle to your dancing while making new friends and getting a great workout! Sign-up now for Alfonso’s Spring Salsa Course starting Jul.14.2015, taught by W.S.F. World Salsa Champion and International Mayan Champion Alfonso Caldera.
